I defended my dissertation on the 8th of July, 2015. My doctoral advisor was Andreas Haeberlen. This is a snapshot of my academic home page as of that date. I now work at Cockroach Labs. These days I mostly hang around on Github and Twitter, so come find me there.

Research

My PhD research was in distributed systems, privacy, and security. I was also interested in research relating to stability of the the financial system.
